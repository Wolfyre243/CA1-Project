My Pledge

Actions I will take to contribute to Zero Hunger

Goal 1: Reduce Food Waste

Ordering / Buying Food

I will strive to order smaller portions of food, so as to not waste the food that I cannot finish.
In the event I do have leftovers, I will try to save them for another day if possible. Managing food waste is essential to ensuring that there is still edible food left for others.

Food waste also comes from grocery products. I will buy what I need only to prevent over-buying and eventually wasting the food. Checking expiry dates are also important to ensure that food is not wasted.
