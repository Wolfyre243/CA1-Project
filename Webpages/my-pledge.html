My Pledge

My Aspirations to achieve Zero Hunger

Goal 1: Reduce Food Waste

Ordering / Buying Food

I will strive to order smaller portions of food, so as to not waste the food that I cannot finish.
In the event I do have leftovers, I will try to save them for another day if possible. Managing food waste is essential to ensuring that there is still edible food left for others.

Food waste also comes from grocery products. I will buy what I need only to prevent over-buying and eventually wasting the food. Checking expiry dates are also important to ensure that food is not wasted.

Goal 2: Donation Drives

An elderly couple receving a care package

Donating to Food from the Heart

I will make an effort to donate any food that I may not need to organisations such as Food From the Heart

Food from the Heart organises food donation drives and helps to deliver these care packages to those in need. Although not a global scale, doing so still helps to work towards the Zero Hunger SDG by battling hunger itself here in Singapore.

Some organisations also occasionally organise community services where people cook for the needy. During my free time, I can also volunteer to help and deliver food to people.

Goal 3: Choose Local Produce

Buy Sustainable and Local Products

As my last goal, I will choose to buy locally grown produce in order to support the development of sustainable food production.
Sustainable produce is important to achieve Zero Hunger as a sustainable source of food ensures that there is enough food for everyone.

This can mitigate the effects of World Hunger and put us one step closer to achieving Zero Hunger.
